About this project

CampusTutor VUT is a student project built for the AIWEX3A Web Management module at the Vaal University of Technology. The site is designed and maintained by a small team of third-year IT students who experienced the same problem we are trying to solve: it is hard to find a senior peer who can explain a tricky module before an upcoming test.
